About Karanja Lad 🛕

Karanja Lad is a town located in Washim district of Maharashtra, known for its deep spiritual roots and communal harmony 🕊. It is considered a holy place as it is the birthplace of Shri Nrusimha Saraswati Swami Maharaj — the second avatar of Lord Dattatreya.

The town is surrounded by temples, mosques, and cultural landmarks that reflect its long-standing history of peace and devotion 🌿. People from all walks of life visit Karanja to experience its calm environment, heritage, and teachings.

Famous Places 📍

Shri Swami Math 🛕

This is the birthplace and sacred math of Shri Nrusimha Saraswati Maharaj. Devotees visit here from across India to seek blessings and meditate in peace.

Gomukh Kund 💧

A holy water reservoir near the Math, believed to be a source of spiritual purification. Many pilgrims bathe here before darshan.

Guru Mandir 🕯

Close to Swami Math, this mandir honors the guru tradition. Visitors come here to reflect and gain spiritual guidance.

Jama Masjid 🕌

A historical mosque representing the religious harmony of Karanja. It is known for its architecture and peaceful atmosphere.

Hanuman Mandir 🙌

Dedicated to Lord Hanuman, this temple is believed to grant strength and protection. Locals visit it daily for blessings.

Datta Mandir 🧘‍♂

A peaceful temple of Lord Dattatreya, ideal for meditation and spiritual practice. It is surrounded by nature and calm vibes.

Buddha Vihar 🕊

A serene Buddhist place of worship in Karanja that symbolizes peace, equality, and modern harmony.

Vithoba Temple 🎶

Devoted to Lord Vitthal, this temple is filled with bhakti and music, especially during festivals like Ashadi Ekadashi.

Jain Temple 🌼

An ancient Digambar Jain temple known for its stone carvings and calm spiritual environment for Jain followers.

Our Culture ✨

Karanja Lad is a town of tradition, devotion, and unity 🌸. The local culture blends spiritual teachings with vibrant social life across communities.

Major festivals like Datta Jayanti, Hanuman Jayanti, Ashadi Ekadashi, and Navratri are celebrated with bhajans, rangoli 🪔, kirtans, and colorful gatherings.

During Navratri , locals enjoy Garba nights, diya decorations, and cultural events wearing traditional attire. The spiritual energy during these days fills the town with light, music, and devotion.

Across all faiths and communities, Karanja Lad celebrates life together — with peace, music, and mutual respect 🤝.
